CASE STUDY OF EMV EMPOWERED BANKING MOBILE APP

A NEW WAY OF BANKING FOR MILLENNIALS

EMV Empowered Banking was created for millennials. After intensive research we found many community banks
that have traditionally focused upon local customers who bank at the brick and mortar branches, have noticed the number of
new customers from the generation of consumers described as the “Millennials (Ages 24-39)” has dwindled significantly.
How can we create a mobile banking APP to fit in the lifestyle and needs of the younger generations?
